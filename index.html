IN COMMEMORATION OF 2020

Curated by Gloria Yang & Icy Lin

The exhibition consists of the work of ten students from UC Berkeley and is divided into four main galleries. The four main themes are COVID-19, Black Lives Matter, Climate Change, and The Election. Most of the people in the world have experienced these events to some extent. Hopefully this exhibition will resonate and make a difference in the world.

Students at Berkeley have created a series of works inspired by what happened this year. Some of these works are 2D forms, some are 3D, some are traditional media, and some new media.
